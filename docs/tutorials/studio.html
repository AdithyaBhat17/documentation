Document Cognition​

Document Cognition | Sharepoint Connector​

Sharepoint connector is now available on the Yellow.ai platform - the world's leading CX Automation Platform. Dive into a demo to understand how Sharepoint can be used as a connector to index the files/folders stored in SharePoint and perform a natural language search across the files/folders. Big Data? No problem!

Document Cognition | AWS Connector​

AWS connector is now available on the Yellow.ai platform - the world's leading CX Automation Platform. Dive into a demo to understand how AWS S3 can be used as a connector to index the files/folders stored in S3 buckets and perform natural language search across the files/folders. Big data? No problem!

Lifecycle management​

See how yellow.ai carries out #BLM as the bots move from the environments of Sandbox to Staging to Production. Use configurable workflows through the 3 stages to ensure zero data loss with all necessary approvals & logs in place.

Learning and continous improvement​

Never let an intent or multiple conflicting intents go unnoticed. See how yellow.ai uses supervised learning (as inputs from agent actions) and unsupervised learning (Feedback based Active Self-Learning where users are offered smart suggestions based on degree of confidence) models to further fine tune bot responses for a superior customer experience.

Pre-built domain models​

Why make a #bot from scratch when you can take advantage of yellow.ai's expertise in domain (be HR, ITSM, Finance, SCM etc) specific intents and entities and utilise pre-built workflows to get your business up & running that much faster.

Pre-built industry models​

Go live in a short span of time with yellow.ai's Industry Specific #Bots where you can access prebuilt NLU models, intents, dialogues and integrations and much more.

What's more? With yellow.ai, you will also get access to prebuilt workflows for industry specific use cases and you will also be able to customise them to suit your CX needs!
