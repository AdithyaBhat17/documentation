## Scope of Integration

Yellow.ai Integration with Zoho CRM allows you to seamlessly connect your Zoho CRM instance with yellow.ai platform. Any customer who has a Zoho CRM account will be able to connect their Zoho instance with yellow.ai using OAuth. This connector will make sure you can do actions on your leads. Using this integration you can create, update, read leads and also you can search for leads with email and phone number.


## Use-cases

Following are the use-cases which are currently accommodated in the Integration:

### Simple integration with Oauth

Yellow.ai does not store the client’s credentials and leverages oAuth 2.0 approach for integrating with client’s Zoho account.

While integrating, yellow.ai navigates the user has to login into his Zoho CRM account and has to provide his consent.



### Do actions with Zoho action nodes

   You can create, update and search all kinds of custom and standard records supported by ZOHO CRM.

## Configuration

Configuring the integration with zoho CRM is straightforward. Follow the steps defined below

to start integrating:

### Navigate to integration tab

Inside your project, navigate to the Configuration tab and then click on the Integrations
tab. Search for Zoho CRM.  

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/Ue3HDlB.png" alt="drawing" width="60%"/>




### Connect your Zoho account

Click on **Connect** to see an option of click to **Connect with Zoho-CRM**. After you click that, you will notice that you will be prompted to login to your Zoho account.

![](https://i.imgur.com/cyNeiG9.gif)


Following the above steps will connect your Zoho CRM account with yellow.ai platform.


# Supported Version

This integration will work on versions 2.0 and above.  
For more information about action nodes you use here, refer this [doc](https://www.zoho.com/crm/developer/docs/api/v2/insert-records.html)
