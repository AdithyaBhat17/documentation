Integrate your knowledge base with google tag manager(GTM)

You can connect GTM to your knowledgebase from the knowledgebase settings page.

Google Tag Manager(GTM) is a tag management system where you can quickly and easily update measurement codes and their related code fragments collectively known as tags on your website or mobile app. Instead of integrating and monitoring tracking code for tools like Google Analytics, Facebook pixel tracking, Google Ads, etc, you can create one GTM tag and monitor all the required tools in one place.

Google Tag Manager will be an intermediate between your knowledge base and the tools(tags) you want to integrate.

See the tag template types(tools) that are natively supported by Google Tag Manager here.

Connect GTM to inbox knowledge base​

To integrate GTM with your knowledge base, follow the steps below:

  1. Create a GTM account. Configure all the tools (required for your KB) like Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook pixel tracking, etc. in your GTM account.

  2. Copy the GTM ID/ Tag tracking code from your GTM account (found on the top bar).

    Learn how.

  1. On yellow.ai platform. Open Inbox > Settings. Search and select Knowledge base.

  2. Click View next to integrations.

  3. Select Google tag manager.

  4. Click Connect.

  5. Enter/paste your GTM ID (step#2).

    (Your GTM ID starts with GTM- followed by an alphanumeric value)

  6. Manually verify if your GTM ID is correct. Click Connect.

    You can log in to your GTM account and verify the integration.

Debug GTM ID/ Delete GTM integration on KB​

  1. If the GTM ID is incorrect, you must Disconnect GTM from the Chatbot integration page and add the new(corrected) GTM ID again(step #7).

You can Disconnect/Connect GTM account to your knowledge base as and when required.

Connect Google analytics to GTM​

This is one example of how to configure tools(Google analytics-GA) in your GTM account. Follow the below steps to connect GA to GTM account:

  1. Copy the Measurement ID for your GA4 property from your google analytics account.

    Sample format: G-A2ABC2ABCD

  2. Create a GTM account by entering Account name, Container name(the URL your GA is configured for) and Container type(Web). 

![](https://i.imgur.com/rnDx7eq.png)

  1. After logging into the Tag manager, you will be directed to the Workspace tab. Click Add a new tag.

  2. Click Tag configuration.

  3. Select Google Analytics: GA4 Configuration (GA4 is the latest version).

  4. Add Measurement ID(Step #1).

  5. Click Triggering. Select Initialization - All Pages. This will activate GA events for all the pages on your URL, you can add advance settings to customize this further.

  6. Click Save.

  7. To debug, click Preview. Add your URL and debug the configuration.
